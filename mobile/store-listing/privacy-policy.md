## Introduction

IOPPS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' ("you" or "your") personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the IOPPS mobile application (the "App").

IOPPS is a platform designed to connect Indigenous peoples with employment opportunities, scholarships, conferences, and Indigenous-owned businesses.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

**Account Information:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- Password (encrypted)

**Profile Information:**
- Professional summary
- Work experience
- Education history
- Skills and qualifications
- Profile picture (optional)
- Location (city, state/province, country)

**Resume and Application Materials:**
- Resume files (PDF, DOC, DOCX)
- Cover letters
- Portfolio materials
- Other documents you upload

**Job Application Information:**
- Jobs you apply to
- Application dates and status
- Communication with employers

**Preferences:**
- Job search preferences
- Notification settings
- Saved jobs and favorites
- Search history

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

**Usage Information:**
- App features accessed
- Pages viewed
- Time spent in the App
- Interaction with content
- Search queries
- Clicks and navigation patterns

**Location Information:**
- Approximate location (city/region) based on IP address
- Precise location (if you grant permission) for location-based job matching

### 1.3 Analytics Information

We use Firebase Analytics and other analytics services to collect:
- App performance metrics
- Crash reports and diagnostics
- User engagement statistics
- Feature usage patterns
- Session duration and frequency

This information is collected in an aggregated and anonymized format when possible.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Update

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Update your profile and account information
- Correct inaccurate data
- View your application history

You can access and modify most information directly in the App settings.

### 5.2 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format. Contact us at [privacy@iopps.app] to request your data.

### 5.3 Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of your personal information by:
- Deleting your account through the App settings
- Contacting us at [privacy@iopps.app]

Note: Some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes after account deletion.

### 5.4 Opt-Out of Communications

You can control communications by:
- Adjusting notification settings in the App
- Unsubscribing from emails using the link in each message
- Contacting us to opt out of specific communications

You cannot opt out of essential service communications (e.g., security alerts, policy changes).

### 5.5 Location Permissions

You can control location access by:
- Adjusting location permissions in your device settings
- Disabling location services in the App

Disabling location may limit some features like location-based job matching.

### 5.6 Analytics Opt-Out

While we use analytics to improve the App, you can:
- Limit ad tracking in your device settings
- Disable analytics data collection (if available in App settings)
