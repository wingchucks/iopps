# AIIC × IOPPS Entrepreneur Awareness Campaign

**Prepared for:** Alberta Indian Investment Corporation / AIIC  
**Programs:** IYE — Indigenous Youth Entrepreneurs; IWE — Indigenous Women Entrepreneurs  
**Prepared by:** Nathan Arias, IOPPS.CA — Empowering Indigenous Success  
**Contract type:** Custom annual awareness campaign, separate from public IOPPS posting plans

---

## Proposed contract

**AIIC Entrepreneur Awareness Campaign**  
**$7,500 + GST**  
**12-month custom annual awareness contract**  
**Invoice split:** 50% IWE / 50% IYE

This proposal is not the IOPPS Premium jobs plan. The public $2,500 Premium plan is for job postings and does not include unlimited program postings. This AIIC contract is a custom awareness campaign focused on promotion, visibility, content, and reporting for AIIC's entrepreneur supports.

---

## Purpose

IOPPS will help Indigenous entrepreneurs across Alberta discover AIIC's financing, youth entrepreneur, women entrepreneur, training, mentorship, and business development supports through IOPPS.ca, IOPPS social/community channels, interview/feature content, Pow Wow Trail visibility, and monthly reporting.

---

## Included services

### 1. Dedicated AIIC resource profile

IOPPS creates and maintains a public AIIC resource profile on IOPPS.ca.

Included:

- AIIC overview
- IYE overview
- IWE overview
- SEAP / AIIC program mention if approved by AIIC
- eligibility notes
- services and support categories
- official website link
- official booking/contact links
- clear entrepreneur call-to-action

### 2. Alberta entrepreneur support placement

AIIC is positioned as a key Alberta entrepreneur support resource in the IOPPS Entrepreneur Supports hub.

Included categories:

- Funding & Loans
- Youth Entrepreneurship
- Women Entrepreneurs
- Training
- Mentorship
- Community Economic Development

### 3. IOPPS Entrepreneur Supports hub visibility

AIIC receives visibility inside the broader IOPPS entrepreneur resource area so founders can find support by province and support type.

### 4. Monthly organic social/community promotion

Each month, IOPPS creates and publishes:

- 1 AIIC-focused post or graphic
- 1 entrepreneur-support mention that includes AIIC
- 1 call-to-action directing entrepreneurs to AIIC's IOPPS resource profile, official website, or booking link

Channels may include:

- Facebook
- Instagram
- LinkedIn, where access allows
- IOPPS.ca
- newsletter/community email when active

### 5. Two larger campaign pushes

IOPPS runs two larger AIIC awareness pushes during the contract year.

Possible campaign themes:

- Indigenous Youth Entrepreneur supports
- Indigenous Women Entrepreneur supports
- Financing and business development help through AIIC
- Book a conversation with AIIC

Each push includes:

- one campaign theme
- up to 3 organic social posts
- IOPPS.ca featured update/block
- newsletter/community mention when active
- simple post-campaign reporting

### 6. One interview / feature package

IOPPS includes one AIIC interview or Q&A feature package for the year.

Included:

- one interview/Q&A session with AIIC staff/team
- basic filming/interview support if Nat is attending in person
- one short IOPPS.ca article or feature summary
- 2–3 clips or quote cards where source footage/content supports it
- social/community promotion for the feature

This is a basic feature package. Full documentary production, multi-camera commercial video, travel-heavy production, or major edited video campaigns are quoted separately.

### 7. One Pow Wow Trail sponsored segment opportunity

IOPPS includes one sponsor-style Pow Wow Trail segment opportunity connected to entrepreneur awareness, youth entrepreneurship, women entrepreneurship, or community economic development.

Included:

- one AIIC sponsor-style mention/segment opportunity
- one supporting social post
- CTA to AIIC's IOPPS resource profile or official booking/contact link

Additional Pow Wow Trail sponsorships, live event coverage, travel, or livestream sponsorships are quoted separately.

### 8. Monthly reporting

IOPPS provides monthly reporting in plain language.

Included where available:

- AIIC profile/page views
- clicks to AIIC website or booking link
- posts published
- social reach/engagement
- newsletter/community mention summary
- recommendations for the next month

### 9. Quarterly planning/check-in review

IOPPS includes up to four light planning reviews during the year to keep messaging aligned.
